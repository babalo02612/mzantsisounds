About Us

mZantsi.sOunds is a South African music streaming platform built with love for local and global music lovers. Our mission is to connect fans with their favorite genres, artists, and undiscovered sounds. Whether you're into Hip Hop, Soul, Gqom, House, or Afro-Pop — we’ve got something for you.

Stream your favorite songs, explore curated playlists, discover new releases, or upload your own tracks. We're all about empowering artists and creating a smooth, immersive listening experience for everyone.

Features

  • 🎧 Stream music by genre or search by song title
  • 🚀 Explore new releases and recommendations
  • 📤 Upload your own tracks and share them with the world
  • 👤 Create playlists and manage your music profile
  • 🔐 Secure user authentication with Firebase
